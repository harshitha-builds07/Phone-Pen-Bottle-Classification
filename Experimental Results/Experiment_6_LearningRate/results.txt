Experiment: Reduced Learning Rate (0.001 → 0.0005)

Training Accuracy: 90%
Validation Accuracy: 70.97%

Training Loss: 0.3224
Validation Loss: 1.3465

Observation:
Since the previous experiment gave a good balance between validation accuracy, validation loss and overfitting, I reduced the learning rate from 0.001 to 0.0005 hoping that the model would learn more carefully and improve validation accuracy.

However, the validation accuracy decreased and the overall performance did not improve compared to the previous experiment.

This experiment showed me that reducing the learning rate does not always lead to better results. For my dataset, the original learning rate of 0.001 worked better.
