Experiment: L2 Regularization

Training Accuracy: 91.2%
Validation Accuracy: 61.29%

Training Loss: 0.3251
Validation Loss: 2.7599

Observation:
Since increasing dropout did not work as expected , I added L2 regularization hoping it would reduce overfitting and improve the model's performance on unseen data.

However, the validation accuracy was lower than the baseline model and the validation loss increased significantly. This showed that L2 regularization was not effective for my dataset
