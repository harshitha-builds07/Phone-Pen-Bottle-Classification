Experiment: Reduced Batch Size (8 → 7)

Training Accuracy: 83.2%
Validation Accuracy: 74.19%

Training Loss: 0.4078
Validation Loss: 0.8697

Observation:
Since reducing the number of epochs gave a better balance between validation accuracy, validation loss and overfitting, I reduced the batch size from 8 to 7. I expected the additional randomness during training to help the model generalize better.

Although this experiment did not achieve the highest validation accuracy, it showed less overfitting, a more stable validation loss and still maintained good validation accuracy.

For my dataset, this experiment gave the best overall balance between validation accuracy, validation loss and generalization. This was my best performing experiment overall.
