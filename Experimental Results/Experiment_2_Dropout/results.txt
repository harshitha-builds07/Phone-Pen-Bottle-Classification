Experiment: Increased Dropout (0.5 → 0.65)

Training Accuracy: 88%
Validation Accuracy: 61.29%

Training Loss: 0.3441
Validation Loss: 1.6776

Observation:
I increased the dropout value from 0.5 to 0.65 expecting it to reduce overfitting and improve validation performance.

However, the validation accuracy dropped from the baseline model and the validation loss remained high. This showed that increasing dropout too much caused the model to lose important information during training.
