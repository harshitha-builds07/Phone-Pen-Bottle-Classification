Experiment: Reduced Epochs (15 → 10)

Training Accuracy: 73.6%
Validation Accuracy: 64.52%

Training Loss: 0.6500
Validation Loss: 0.9812

Observation:
I reduced the number of epochs from 15 to 10 because I felt the model was looking at the training dataset too much and becoming overfitted.

The validation loss became much more stable and the gap between training and validation accuracy also reduced. This showed that reducing the number of epochs helped reduce overfitting.
